Kaya Natin
Youth — Caloocan City

Kaya Natin Youth — Caloocan City is a youth-led organization dedicated to cultivating principled leadership among young people. It promotes good governance, transparency, and accountability, empowering the youth to become active contributors to sustainable and ethical nation-building.

Mission

Empower the youth to become active contributors to ethical and sustainable nation-building.

Vision

To cultivate principled leadership among young people in Caloocan City and beyond.
